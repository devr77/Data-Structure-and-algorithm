To help the students further, we are providing a complimentary TA based doubt support for this course, you can use Q-A section to ask doubts related to concepts, however you should keep some basic guidelines in mind while posting questions.

Guidelines

Do's

Ask for Hints
Ask for Correct Solution / Pseudocode
Ask for Expected Space Time Complexity
Ask for problem statement clarification
Ask for feedback on your algorithm

Share feedback on our teaching


Don'ts
 Don't ask TA to debug/fix your code
Don't ask for solutions to external problems in Q/A Section
Don't spam the Q-A section with new problem/content requests
